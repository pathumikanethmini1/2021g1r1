Reasons for us to use the passive voice
01) When the subject is unknown
a. My purse has been stolen.
b. Her money had been hidden in the room.
c. A man has been killed at the junction.

02) When the subject is well known
a. I have been selected to university.
b. Lunch was prepared by 1.00
c. We will be informed tomorrow morning.

03) When the subject is not important
a. Wash room is being cleaned.
b. They were being taught.
c. The gate has been closed.
